Procedural World Generation
Palace Engine C++ IA GitHub • Jan 2023 - May 2023
World generation with multiagent system. Made by me
What I did
  • World generation using Cellular Automata, with world seeds and three different sizes (small, medium, big)
  • Camera to zoom in and out and followed the "red box" (entity that the player move to)
  • Multi Agent System for animals states (calm, scape and persecution)
  • Random movement for animals calm state
  • A* algorithm for player movement and animals scape and persecution movement
  • Markov Chain for Plants grows and Tiles updates
